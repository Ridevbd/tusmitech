Ridwan Tusmiah

Software Engineer & Content Creator — Founder, TusmiTech

I’m Ridwan Tusmiah, founder of TusmiTech — a hybrid creative-tech studio based in Chattogram, Bangladesh. I build production-ready web applications using Laravel on the backend and Vue.js on the frontend, and I create cinematic visual content that helps brands tell engaging stories. My work sits at the intersection of clean code, scalable systems, and purposeful storytelling.

Over the years I have delivered projects ranging from custom POS systems and e-commerce platforms to CMS-driven museum websites and cinematic event films. I focus on pragmatic engineering: secure, maintainable, and optimized solutions that solve real business problems while keeping user experience front and center.

At TusmiTech I also offer media and growth services — professional photography, video production, content planning, and social channel management — so clients can launch, scale, and sustain a strong online presence without juggling multiple vendors.

Services

Web Development — Smart, Scalable & Built for Business Growth

Custom Web Applications — We create powerful, tailor-made web applications using Laravel (backend) and Vue.js (frontend). Whether it’s a management dashboard, online booking system, CRM, or a custom solution for your business, we build secure, fast, and scalable apps that fit your exact needs — no unnecessary features, just what works for you.

POS (Point of Sale) Software Development

Run your business smoothly with a fully custom POS system. We develop cloud-based POS software that helps you manage sales, track inventory, handle billing, and generate real-time reports — all from one intuitive dashboard. Features include product & inventory management, customer tracking & billing, barcode scanning support, multi-user & multi-branch systems, and real-time sales analytics.

E-Commerce Website Development

Beautiful, user-friendly stores with secure payments, product management, and order tracking. Mobile-optimized and built to convert.

SaaS Development & PWAs

Turn ideas into full-featured SaaS platforms with subscription management, payments, and responsive design. Or opt for PWAs to deliver fast, app-like experiences without the app store.

Website Maintenance & Optimization
