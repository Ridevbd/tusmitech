Ridwan Tusmiah

Software Engineer & Content Creator — Founder, TusmiTech

I’m Ridwan Tusmiah, founder of TusmiTech — a hybrid creative-tech studio based in Chattogram, Bangladesh. I build production-ready web applications using Laravel on the backend and Vue.js on the frontend, and I create cinematic visual content that helps brands tell engaging stories. My work sits at the intersection of clean code, scalable systems, and purposeful storytelling.

Over the years I have delivered projects ranging from custom POS systems and e-commerce platforms to CMS-driven museum websites and cinematic event films. I focus on pragmatic engineering: secure, maintainable, and optimized solutions that solve real business problems while keeping user experience front and center.

At TusmiTech I also offer media and growth services — professional photography, video production, content planning, and social channel management — so clients can launch, scale, and sustain a strong online presence without juggling multiple vendors.
